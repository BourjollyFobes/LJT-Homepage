Abstract
-------
C-Eval is a carefully designed large-scale Chinese evaluation suite that covers 52 different disciplines across 4 difficulty levels, ranging from high school fundamental knowledge to professional advanced domain expertise. It serves as a comprehensive benchmark for evaluating the general knowledge understanding, reasoning, and professional domain capabilities of Chinese large foundation models.
