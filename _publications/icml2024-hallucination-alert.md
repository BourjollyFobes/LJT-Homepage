Abstract
-------
This work presents a novel hallucination mitigation framework that leverages the in-context sharpness property of inner model hidden representations to detect generation hallucinations in real time, without requiring external retrieval resources. Extensive experiments show that our method effectively identifies hallucination tokens and significantly reduces the hallucination rate of large language models on various generation tasks.
