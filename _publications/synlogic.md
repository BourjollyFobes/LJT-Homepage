Abstract
-------
We introduce SynLogic, a scalable framework for synthesizing large amounts of verifiable logical reasoning data to enhance the reasoning capabilities of large language models. The generated datasets are fully verifiable to ensure the correctness of reasoning paths and final answers, leading to significant improvements in the model's logical reasoning performance.
