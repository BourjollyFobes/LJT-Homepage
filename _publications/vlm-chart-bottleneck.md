Abstract
-------
This work systematically investigates the limitations of existing vision-language models on chart understanding tasks, identifying that the core bottleneck lies in the perception stage rather than the subsequent reasoning or decoding stages. We propose targeted optimization strategies that significantly improve the chart understanding performance of existing VLMs.
