Abstract
-------
This work proposes a novel lightweight paradigm for composing parameter-efficient modules (PEMs) through simple arithmetic operations in the parameter space. Our method allows users to combine multiple task-specific PEMs to get composable multi-task models, transfer knowledge across different tasks, and perform new task composition, without requiring full model fine-tuning or extra training data.
